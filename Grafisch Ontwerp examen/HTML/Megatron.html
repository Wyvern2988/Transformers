Megatron

Faction: Decepticons

Role: Leader of the Decepticons

First Appearance: Transformers (2007)

Megatron is the ruthless and powerful leader of the Decepticons. His goal is to conquer Cybertron and destroy the Autobots at all costs. Once a close ally of Optimus Prime, Megatron turned against him when their ideologies clashed, leading to an endless war that spread to Earth.

Known for his intelligence, cruelty, and ambition, Megatron is one of the most iconic villains in the Transformers universe. His alternate forms include a Cybertronian jet, a tank, and sometimes even a handgun.
